Base.vn|Brand Context

Base is a comprehensive business-management SaaS for Vietnamese companies — but "comprehensive" had become a ceiling.

Brand

A "do-everything" position that is too broad — not top-of-mind for any one job, sub-brands blurred together.

Penetration

Low SME digital maturity and long sales cycles; SMEs default to cheap, short-term fixes.

Competition

Undercut by cheaper, specialized players (MISA, 1Office, FastWork) on single jobs.

Target: SMEs under 500 employees that need digital transformation. The product suite spans Work+, HRM+, Finance+ and Info+ — but leading with breadth invited a price fight Base couldn't win.

Market trend · Ken Research, 11/2024

Vietnam's business-software market is accelerating on the back of a national digital-transformation push — reaching ~156M USD, with SMEs actively investing in tech.

Key finding

The market is expanding and largely uncontested. Huge room to grow — and to bring AI into the operating workflow before competitors own that story.

Base.vn|Strategy · Job-to-be-Done

Lead with Work+. Win workflow first, expand the ecosystem after.

Big Idea — "Workflow by Base: operating software for Vietnamese leaders."

Key message: "One touch, operations optimized." Work+ is the entry point to win the workflow job; customization with the wider ecosystem (HRM+, …) follows.

Deepen

Become the default in-category

Make Work+ the first choice for operations. Focus on SMEs of 50–300 staff already outgrowing Excel/Zalo.

KPI · Top-of-mind for "workflow"
Expand

Enter complex verticals

Bring Work+ into industries that lack specialized software — F&B chains, Medical, Retail — with ready-made industry templates.

KPI · Grow the customer portfolio
Leverage

Turn customers into channel

Use the existing customer base for referrals and lookalikes — same industry, same supply chain, CEO/COO networks.

KPI · Build up case studies
