Case 02 · Independent Growth Analysis

From First Booking to Repeat Habit

User JourneyActivationRetentionExperiment Design
Independent portfolio analysis based on publicly observable product experiences. Not an internal AhaMove project.

The user is not booking a driver. They are protecting a customer promise.

Functional job

"Book a suitable delivery fast, so I can fulfil the order without stopping my business."

Emotional job

"Feel confident the delivery won't damage my customer relationship."

Social job

"Look reliable and professional — even with a team of one."

Persona: small online seller in HCMC, ~5–20 orders/day. Opportunity: become part of the merchant's daily operating habit, not just a delivery option.

Trust friction

Growth friction

No reason to return · second booking not easier — prevents habit
